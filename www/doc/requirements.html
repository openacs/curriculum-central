Requirements Document

Abstract

This document describes the requirements for the Curriculum Central application. Curriculum Central is an application for professors and teaching staff alike, to organise the curriculum for degree offerings. The package will be integrated with dotLRN, and will offer course management capabilities to the Learning Management System (LMS).
